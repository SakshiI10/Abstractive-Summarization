former heavyweight boxing champion david haye , 34 , was stopped by officials on his arrival at dubai international airport and accused of fraud by writing a cheque as payment for a new property which bounced .
the boxer also handed over his passport to authorities , and has been unable to leave the united arab emirates .
the cheque , worth 1.8 million emirati dirham , was intended as a final payment on a newly-built property , as part of a deal with developers emaar , which is part-owned by the dubai government .
