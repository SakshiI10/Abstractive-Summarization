hemp is used by pet owners to treat their cats and dogs .
there are a rising number of firms in the us that sell biscuits containing cannabidiol extracted from hemp .
two of the most common compounds are tetrahydrocannabinol - the psychoactive chemical that produces the cannabis high - and cannabidiol -lrb- cbd -rrb- .
hemp naturally contains compounds which affect the endocannabinoid system , an internal pathway found in mammals .
