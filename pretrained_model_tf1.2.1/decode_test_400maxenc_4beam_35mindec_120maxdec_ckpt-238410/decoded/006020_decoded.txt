the rev. robert h. schuller , the southern california televangelist and author who beamed his upbeat messages on faith and redemption to millions from his landmark crystal cathedral only to see his empire crumble in his waning years , has died .
schuller died early thursday at a care facility in artesia , daughter carol schuller milner said .
in 2013 , he was diagnosed with a tumor in his esophagus that had spread to his lymph nodes and began treatment .
