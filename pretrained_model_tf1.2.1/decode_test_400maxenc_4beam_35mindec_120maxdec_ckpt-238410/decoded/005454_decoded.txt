rules laid down by transport for london -lrb- tfl -rrb- mean refugees are exempt from criminal records checks .
the legal loophole in an official application document on ` private hire driver licensing ' says people coming to britain will not be required to have their criminal convictions checked .
the form , seen by the daily express , states : ` with regards to overseas criminal records checks , no such checks will be made in respect of those who declare that they are in possession of or who have applied for refugee or asylum status '
